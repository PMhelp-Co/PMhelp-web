ABOUT PMHELP
Unlock Your Future in Product Management For Free
Our mission
What do we do
We offer free, curated resources, expert guidance, and a supportive community to help graduates and career shifters break into product management. From interview prep and CV revamps to practical knowledge and green skills, we equip aspiring PMs with the tools they need to thrive.
Why do we exist
We exist to bridge the gap between ambition and opportunity for aspiring product managers, ensuring that career growth and access to valuable resources are free and available to everyone, regardless of their background.
Our vision
Where do we want to be in the future
Empowering aspiring product managers worldwide with free, accessible resources and expert guidance to launch their careers and shape the future of product management
Product Managers from these companies have found our curated resources helpful
Our Core Values
Accessibility
We believe that everyone should have free access to the knowledge and tools needed to pursue a career in product management, regardless of their background or financial situation.
Community
We foster a supportive and inclusive environment where aspiring product managers can learn, grow, and connect with peers and experts.
Empowerment
We are dedicated to empowering individuals with the skills, confidence, and resources they need to break into and excel in the field of product management.
Inclusion
We believe that talent is equally distributed, and opportunity should not be limited to a select advantaged few. We are committed to creating opportunities for aspiring product managers from all backgrounds.
Innovation
We embrace new ideas and continuously evolve our offerings to ensure that our community stays ahead in the ever-changing world of product management.
Sustainability
We are committed to equipping future product managers with the green skills needed to drive sustainable product development and contribute to the global economy
